Crystal/ Gem Collector

By the Power of Grayskull!!!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four gems below. By clicking on a gem you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

If you match your total score to the random number,

you win!

If your total score goes above the random number,

you lose!

The value of each gem is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the value of each gem will change.

Good luck!
